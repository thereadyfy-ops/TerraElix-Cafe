Our Story

Rooted in soil,
finished by hand.

TerraElix began as a single roasting drum in the back of a neighborhood café. Today, we still roast in micro-batches, small enough that every bag carries the fingerprint of the person who roasted it.

We work directly with growers across three continents, paying above fair-trade rates for beans we'd be proud to serve our own family. It shows up in the cup — bright, clean, and honest.
